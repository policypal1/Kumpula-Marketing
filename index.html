Questions

Things people ask before we start.

How does "$0 down, pay when it's live" actually work?

+

We have a short call. I build your site. You review it on a live preview link. If you love it, we push it to your domain and you pay the agreed price. If you don't love it, you don't pay and we part ways — no hard feelings, no invoices.

What's the catch?

+

No catch. I'd rather invest a few days building something you love than collect a deposit and deliver something you don't. It filters for serious businesses and lets my work speak for itself.

How fast can my website be ready?

+

Usually 5 to 7 business days from our kickoff call. If the project is straightforward, it can move faster.

Do I need to provide content?

+

No. I can write the copy too. You just tell me about your business, what you want to sell, and who you want calling.

What if I want changes before I pay?

+

That's expected. You get a round of revisions before you decide. I want it to be a site you're proud to send people to — if we need tweaks to get there, we'll make them.

Can you help with Google Ads too?

+

Yes, but the website needs to convert first. Ads work a lot better when the landing experience is already strong.
