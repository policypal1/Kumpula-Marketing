Questions

Things people ask before we start.

How does the growth plan work?

+

I look at where leads come from, what customers see online, and where you may be losing business. Then I show you the clearest next step instead of forcing you into a preset package.

What can you help my business with?

+

I can improve the website, strengthen the Google presence, run Google Ads, and track which calls, jobs, and revenue came from the marketing.

Do I need a new website before running ads?

+

Not always. I first review what you already have. If the current site can convert traffic, we can improve it. If it is the bottleneck, I will recommend rebuilding the landing experience before increasing ad spend.

Can you control how many leads I receive?

+

Yes. Campaign budgets, locations, schedules, and service targeting can be adjusted around your capacity so the goal is a manageable number of qualified leads rather than overwhelming your operation.

How do you measure whether the marketing works?

+

I track calls, forms, booked jobs, cost per lead, and revenue when the sales data is available. The question is simple: did the marketing bring the business more work?

How quickly can we launch?

+

A focused campaign or landing-page project can often launch within one to three weeks. Larger website projects may take longer depending on content, integrations, approvals, and scope.
