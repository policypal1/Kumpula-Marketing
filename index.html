Questions

Things people ask before we start.

How do custom growth plans work?

+

We start with a strategy call about your goals, market, current marketing, budget, and workload capacity. I then recommend the highest-leverage plan rather than forcing your business into a preset package.

What can you help my business with?

+

I can help with brand positioning, websites and landing pages, Google Business, local SEO, Google Ads, lead capture, conversion tracking, campaign copy, and ongoing optimization.

Do I need a new website before running ads?

+

Not always. I first review what you already have. If the current site can convert traffic, we can improve it. If it is the bottleneck, I will recommend rebuilding the landing experience before increasing ad spend.

Can you control how many leads I receive?

+

Yes. Campaign budgets, locations, schedules, and service targeting can be adjusted around your capacity so the goal is qualified, manageable growth rather than overwhelming your operation.

How do you measure whether the marketing works?

+

I track the actions that matter, such as calls, forms, booked jobs, cost per lead, and attributed revenue when the sales data is available. Reporting is tied to business outcomes, not vanity metrics alone.

How quickly can we launch?

+

A focused campaign or landing-page project can often launch within one to three weeks. Larger brand and website projects may take longer depending on content, integrations, approvals, and scope.
